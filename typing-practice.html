Tips to Boost Your Speed

  • Master Touch Typing: Keep your eyes on the screen, not your fingers. This is the golden rule.
  • Maintain Good Posture: Sit up straight with your feet flat on the floor. It reduces fatigue and improves focus.
  • Use All Your Fingers: Learn the correct finger placement for each key. Our finger legend helps!
  • Practice Consistently: Just 15-30 minutes of focused practice daily yields significant results.
  • Focus on Accuracy First: Speed will naturally follow accuracy. Don't rush and build bad habits.

Benefits of Faster Typing

  • Increased Productivity: Spend less time typing and more time thinking and creating.
  • Better Job Prospects: Many modern jobs value or require efficient typing skills.
  • Improved Focus: When you can type without thinking, your mind is free to concentrate on your ideas.
  • Reduced Strain: Proper technique reduces the risk of Repetitive Strain Injury (RSI).
  • Enhanced Communication: Respond faster in chats, emails, and online collaborations.
