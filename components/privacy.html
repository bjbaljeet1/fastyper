Privacy Policy for Fastyper

Last Updated: May 21, 2024

Welcome to Fastyper. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website.

Information We Collect (and Don't Collect)

Fastyper is designed to be a privacy-focused platform. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or IP address.

The only data we store is saved directly on your own device's web browser using LocalStorage. This includes:

  • Test History: Your top 10 typing test results (WPM, accuracy, mode) are saved to display your personal leaderboard.
  • Finger Train Progress: Your progress through the Finger Train levels is saved so you can continue where you left off.
  • Cookie Consent: Your acceptance of our cookie/localStorage policy.

This data remains in your browser and is never transmitted to our servers.

How We Use This Information

The data stored in your browser's LocalStorage is used solely to enhance your user experience by providing persistent features like progress tracking and personal leaderboards.

Third-Party Services

To support the operational costs of Fastyper, we use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Your Control Over Your Data

You have full control over the data stored in your browser. You can clear your browser's LocalStorage for this site at any time to remove all saved test history and progress. Fastyper also provides a "Delete History" button on the results page to clear your top scores.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact Us page.
